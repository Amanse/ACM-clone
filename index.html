ACM CHAPTER

About
The Chitkara University ACM Student Chapter is an official student body Chartered on 28 February 2010. This Chapter aims at creating a technomotive environment for the students of the Computer Science field apart from their regular course content. The chapter arranges various resourceful workshops, expert-talks, industry-oriented courses, hands-on workshops, industrial visits, quizzes and coding competitions. With an enormous amount of dedication towards it’s members, this chapter has received the “Global Level ACM Excellence Award 2021 for Outstanding Community Service”. The forte of the chapter is to provide prerequisite knowledge along with competitive coding. With dedication and coorporation of our team along with a constant hunger for perfection, together we strive.
